Allowed users to upload, share and view stories on mobile or web. index_image: /images/lock-in-the-love.png banner_image: /images/lock-in-the-love.png platforms: Web, Mobile technologies: PHP, MySQL, HTML, CSS, JS side_links: Check out a version of the photo wall with photos from the event --- This website was created as part of a 3 day hackathon where my team produced a product for the Heart Foundation. It allowed users to receive an email with a unique upload url where they could upload a photo and a message. This was automatically added to a draggable public photo wall, which others could explore. The website was contructed in a very short timeframe using PHP and MySQL.
